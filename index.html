Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How long does window installation take?

Project timelines vary depending on the products selected, manufacturer availability, and the scope of the installation. Estimated timelines will be provided once the final product selection and order have been confirmed. Our team works closely with trusted manufacturers to ensure projects are completed as efficiently as possible.

How much does window replacement cost?

Cost varies based on window type, size, material, and the number of windows being replaced. We provide free in-home estimates with no pressure — contact us and we'll give you a detailed, written quote tailored to your home.

Do new windows increase home value?

Yes — new windows are one of the best home improvement investments. They improve energy efficiency, curb appeal, and comfort, all of which contribute to higher home value. Energy-efficient windows can also reduce heating and cooling costs year-round.

What window brands do you install?

We work exclusively with reputable manufacturers that stand behind their products with strong warranties. Warranty coverage is determined by the individual manufacturer of each product, and full warranty documentation will be provided at the time of installation. Contact us to discuss which brands best fit your needs and budget.
